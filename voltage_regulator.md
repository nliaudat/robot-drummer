## 12V Voltage regulator and fuse : 

### Concept
[+12 volts in]-->[1 ohm, 5 watt resistor]-->[1N5402 diode]-->[1000uF, 25v cap]  --> [protected 12V]
                                                                               |
                                                                               --> [LM7805 ] --> [clean 5V]
