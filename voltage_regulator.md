## 12V Voltage regulator and fuse : 

### Concept
[+12 volts in]-->[1 ohm, 5 watt resistor]-->[1N5402 diode]-->[1000uF, 25v cap]-->2X [LM7805 ] to get clean 5V, 1x for arduino, 1x for relay module
